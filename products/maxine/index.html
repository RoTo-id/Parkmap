FAQ

How much does Plus cost? +

Plus is on the way and not released yet. It will be a separate paid Android edition — a 29 SEK one-time purchase via Google Play. No subscription, no recurring fees.

Where can I get the Free app? +

Free is available now on Google Play — search for MedRemind or use the Get it on Google Play button above.

Do you sell my data? +

No. There are no analytics SDKs and no data brokers. Your name and settings stay on your device.

Is this a broad AI assistant? +

No. The public framing is a focused workspace for tasks, reminders, drafts, product work, and reviewable outputs.

Is it available on iPhone? +

Android first. iOS is on the roadmap.

Is it available in my language? +

We currently ship in English, Swedish, Spanish, German, French, Italian, and Portuguese.

How does Plus differ from Free? +

Plus removes ads, adds expanded overview space, more reminder slots, and a calmer priority-ready review flow.
