The idea

One button. That's the whole app.

Most medicine apps over-engineer the problem. MedRemind does the opposite: a countdown timer, a confirmation button, and — if you want — an e-mail to a family member when you press it.

No account sign-up. No streak mechanics. No analytics tracking you across sessions. The app does one thing and stays out of the way.

FAQ

How much does Plus cost? +

MedRemind Plus is a one-time purchase of 29 SEK (about €2.50) via Google Play. No subscription.

Do you sell my data? +

No. There are no analytics SDKs and no data brokers. Your name and settings stay on your device.

What about the family e-mail? +

Only triggered if you enter an e-mail and press the confirmation button. The recipient address and a brief message are sent via the Resend API. No history is stored on our side.

Does MedRemind replace a doctor or a medical device? +

No. MedRemind is a reminder tool only and does not verify that medication was actually taken. Always consult your doctor.

Will reminders work if the phone is off or muted? +

Reminders fire while the phone is on. If muted, vibration and screen wake are used.

Is it available on iPhone? +

Android first. iOS is on the roadmap.

Is it available in my language? +

We currently ship in English, Swedish, Spanish, German, French, Italian, and Portuguese.

How does Plus differ from Free visually? +

Plus removes all ads and replaces the modal popup with a lightweight toast notification. Multi-dose mode also unlocks three separate reminder slots.

Medical disclaimer

This app is a reminder tool only and does not verify that medication was actually taken. Always consult your doctor. MedRemind is not a medical device.
