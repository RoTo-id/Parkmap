MedRemind

Peace of mind in one button.

A reminder on their phone. An optional confirmation for you.

MedRemind is a one-button Android reminder and check-in app for families. The user presses the button when the reminder appears, the app shows the next 24-hour countdown, and if a family e-mail has been added, a short confirmation can be sent after the tap.

The idea

Two people, one button.

MedRemind keeps the daily reminder moment deliberately small: a visible countdown timer, one confirmation button, and — if the user has added a family e-mail — a short note to that family member when the button is pressed.

No account. No streak mechanics. No Maxine analytics. The user gets a reminder they can actually use; the family gets an optional check-in signal without turning the app into supervision.

FAQ

How much does Plus cost? +

MedRemind Plus is a one-time purchase of 29 SEK (about €2.50) via Google Play. No subscription.

Do you sell my data? +

No. There are no analytics SDKs and no data brokers. Your name and settings stay on your device.

What about the family e-mail? +

Only triggered if you enter an e-mail and press the confirmation button. The recipient address and a brief message are sent via the Resend API. No history is stored on our side.

Will reminders work if the phone is off or muted? +

Reminders fire while the phone is on. If muted, vibration and screen wake are used.

Is it available on iPhone? +

Android first. iOS is on the roadmap.

Is it available in my language? +

We currently ship in English, Swedish, Spanish, German, French, Italian, and Portuguese.

How does Plus differ from Free visually? +

Plus removes all ads and replaces the modal popup with a lightweight toast notification. Plus also unlocks three separate reminder slots.
