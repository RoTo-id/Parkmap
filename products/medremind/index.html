The idea

Two people, one button.

Most medicine apps over-engineer the problem. MedRemind does the opposite: a countdown timer, a confirmation button, and — if the user has added a family e-mail — a short note to that family member when the button is pressed.

No account. No Maxine analytics. No data brokers. Reminder settings stay on the phone. The free tier may show Google AdMob ads; Plus removes ads. The parent gets a reminder they can actually use; the family member gets a confirmation signal without turning every reminder into a call. That's the whole app.

FAQ

How much does Plus cost? +

MedRemind Plus is a separate paid Android app/edition on Google Play. It costs 29 SEK as a one-time purchase. No subscription.

Do you sell my data? +

No. We do not use our own analytics SDKs, sell data, or use data brokers. Reminder settings stay on your device. The free tier may show Google AdMob ads; Plus removes ads. Optional family e-mail is sent only when you enter an address and press the confirmation button.

What about the family e-mail? +

Only triggered if you enter an e-mail and press the confirmation button. The recipient address and a brief message are sent via the Resend API. No history is stored on our side.

Does MedRemind replace a doctor, caregiver, or emergency service? +

No. MedRemind is a reminder and check-in tool only. It is not a medical device, does not verify that medication was actually taken, and does not replace medical advice, caregivers, emergency services, or normal family contact.

Will reminders work if the phone is off or muted? +

Reminders fire while the phone is on. If muted, vibration and screen wake are used.

Is it available on iPhone? +

Android first. iOS is on the roadmap.

Is it available in my language? +

We currently ship in English, Swedish, Spanish, German, French, Italian, and Portuguese.

How does Plus differ from Free visually? +

Plus removes all ads and replaces the modal popup with a lightweight toast notification. Multi-dose mode also unlocks three separate reminder slots.
