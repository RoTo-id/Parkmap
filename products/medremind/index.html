The problem

Roughly 1 in 3 people forget doses regularly.

Forgetting medicine isn't a moral failing — it's a system failure. Phones notify us about everything except the one thing our health actually depends on. Generic reminder apps get closed, snoozed, and forgotten.

MedRemind does one thing well: it fires, it waits, and it logs whether you took the dose. That's it. No gamification, no streaks, no account.

What MedRemind does

Never miss a dose

Reliable local notifications that fire even when the app is closed or the phone is sleeping.

Flexible schedules

Daily, weekly, every-X-days, or specific weekdays. Multiple times per day. Dose amount per entry.

Caregiver sharing (Plus)

Invite a family member or caregiver to see status and confirmations in real time.

History + export

See which doses were taken, missed, or snoozed. Export a CSV for your doctor.

Works offline

All reminder logic runs on-device. No account required for the free tier.

Privacy-first

Your medicine data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly enable caregiver sharing.

FAQ

How much does it cost? +

The core reminder app is free, forever. Plus — with caregiver sharing, advanced history, and export — is a one-time purchase or monthly subscription.

Do you sell my data? +

No. There are no ads, no trackers, and no analytics SDKs. Your medicine data stays on your device.

Will reminders work if the phone is off or muted? +

Reminders fire as long as the phone is on. If muted, we use vibration + screen wake. You can enable critical-alerts in settings.

Can I share with my partner or parent? +

Yes — with MedRemind Plus. You invite them by email and they see confirmations in real time.

Is this GDPR-compliant? +

Yes. Built in Sweden under EU data-protection law. Data processed on-device unless you opt into sharing.

Will this drain my battery? +

Negligible — we use the OS-native alarm scheduler, not a background process.

Is it available on iPhone? +

Android first (launching this month). iOS is on the roadmap.

What languages are supported? +

Swedish and English at launch. More coming based on demand.
